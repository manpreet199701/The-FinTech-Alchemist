About Me

My story isn't a straight line. It starts with a solid foundation in commerce and logistics, an academic path that took me from Delhi University to a diploma in Canada. Along the way, I gained hands-on experience in finance and banking, building a strong understanding of how the business world operates.

But even with that expertise, a childhood fascination with robotics never left me. That same curiosity has since evolved into a powerful drive to explore the dynamic worlds of AI, machine learning, and coding. Now, my mission is to bridge those two worlds. I'm on a journey to build a career that seamlessly blends my knowledge of financial markets—from investments to equity—with the transformative power of artificial intelligence. I believe that by combining these skills, I can create something truly unique and help innovate the future of finance.
