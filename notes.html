Note 1: Understanding LLM Tokenization

Tokenization is the process of breaking down text into smaller units (tokens). These tokens are the fundamental units models like Gemini use to process information. Shorter, common words often get their own token, while complex words are broken down into subwords. This affects cost and model context window usage.

Note 2: Alpha in Quant Trading

Alpha ($\alpha$) represents the excess return of an investment relative to the return of a benchmark index. It's often used as a measure of an investment manager's performance or the quality of a trading strategy. A positive alpha indicates the investment outperformed the market, while a negative alpha indicates underperformance.
